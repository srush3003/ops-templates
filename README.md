# ops-templates

Reusable templates for operators and technical teams.
Each template in this repo documents a real operational
problem, why it exists, and a structured way to prevent
or solve it.

These are not generic starting points. They come from
real engagements, real delays, and real decisions made
under pressure. Use them as-is or adapt them to your
context.

---

## What's in here

| Template | What it solves |
|----------|---------------|
| [`integration-planning-template.md`](https://github.com/srush3003/ops-templates/blob/main/integration-planning-template.md) | Prevents launch delays caused by missing credentials, unclear ownership, and integrations nobody started early enough |

More templates added as frameworks are extracted from
active engagements.

---

## How to use these templates

Each template is self-contained. Open it, read the
explanation at the top, and fill it in during the
relevant meeting or kickoff. They are designed to be
used with your team and client in the same session,
not filled in alone after the fact.

If something does not apply to your project, remove it.
If something is missing for your context, add it and
consider contributing it back.
